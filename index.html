Why Vallée Bras-du-Nord?

Mountain Biking at Vallée Bras-du-Nord

The Shannahan area is the wild side of the Valley. Just 20 minutes from Saint-Raymond, this network of trails cleverly nested among hills of glacial debris brings you deep inside the boreal forest, along vertiginous cliffs, rivers and waterfalls. Two days of riding are possible for beginners as well as experts, with accomodation in camping, yurts or cottages.

The Saint-Raymond sector is the countryside of the Valley. Near the village, in a rural setting, 30 km of well-cut singletracks await you. A succession of short climbs and descents full of flow, grouped on two small mountains, that you ride with a big smile on your face, before enjoying a well-earned pint at the microbrewery at the trailhead.
